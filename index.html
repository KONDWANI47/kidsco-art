About Summary

About Us

Our History

Kidsco Academy was founded to provide a warm, engaging environment where young learners build strong academic and social foundations. Over the years, we've grown our programs while keeping class sizes small and instruction personal.

Small class sizes for personalized attention
Experienced and caring teachers
Play-based learning approach

Mission

We empower every child to become a confident, compassionate, and curious learner through developmentally appropriate teaching, family partnerships, and vibrant school culture.

Our Values

  • • Nurturing individual growth
  • • Building strong foundations
  • • Fostering creativity and curiosity
  • • Creating inclusive communities

Programs Summary

Programs

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten is play-based and child-centered. Learners develop early literacy and numeracy through purposeful play, songs, stories, and guided exploration.

  • Language-rich classrooms and daily read-alouds
  • Hands-on math and science discovery
  • Art, music, and movement woven into routines

Primary: Standards 1–4
