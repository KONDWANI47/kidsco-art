Our Story

How We Started

Kidsco Academy began with a simple belief: every child deserves a joyful, inspiring school where they are seen, heard, and encouraged to grow. A small group of passionate teachers and parents founded Kidsco with a focus on play-based learning in the early years and strong foundations in primary school.

Over the years, we've welcomed families from diverse backgrounds, expanded our programs, and built a vibrant community. Many former students return as mentors and volunteers, sharing how Kidsco shaped their confidence and love of learning.

Family partnerships and community involvement
Creativity through arts, music, and movement
Inquiry-based learning and curiosity

What We Teach

Our curriculum blends literacy, numeracy, science, social studies, expressive arts, and life skills. We also teach social-emotional learning—helping children work together, solve problems, and care for themselves and others.

How Kids' Lives Change

  • • Confidence to speak, read, and solve problems
  • • Healthy routines and teamwork
  • • Curiosity about the world and community
  • • Respect, kindness, and leadership
